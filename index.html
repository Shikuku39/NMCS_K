What is NMCS?

National Movement of Catholic Students- Kenya is a movement of tertiary Catholic students (as its name suggests) brought together for the purpose of serving God and the community. Our motto is Preferential Option for the Poor. Our aim in the community is to promote peace and serve the less fortunate in the community through our activities

OUR ACTIVITIES AND EVENTS

Activities

As a movement, we take part in various activities at chapter level and also at National level. Most of our activities are charity-driven. As a movement, we have adopted more than 15 children nation wide and they are under the care of the respective chapters. To support the children, chapters often organise fund drives to raise money for school fees for the children. Apart from that, our members also take part in tree planting and environmental conservation. Above all, at the end of the day we all come together to pray and fellowship

Events

Our events are mostly organized by the National council (National Executive and the chapter representatives). There are about four national events that are organized every year by the National office.

  • NMCS- Kenya National Mass
  • NMCS- Kenya Pre-Easter Camp
  • NMCS- Kenya Alumni Mass and fundraiser
  • NMCS- Kenya Biennial National Conference

The most favorite this year was the NMCS-K and TMCS joint Pre-Easter Camp held at Kisii University chapter.
Other events that are organized leadership trainings which are mostly regional.
